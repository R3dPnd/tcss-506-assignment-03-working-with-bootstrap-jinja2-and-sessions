About TripAdvisor Explorer

Application Purpose

TripAdvisor Explorer is a web application designed to help users discover and explore popular destinations, restaurants, and attractions in cities across the United States. Using TripAdvisor data, the application provides an intuitive interface to browse through various categories of businesses and locations, complete with photos, addresses, and direct links to their websites.

Features

  • Browse businesses by category
  • View business details including photos and addresses
  • Access direct links to business websites
  • Interactive carousel interface for easy navigation
